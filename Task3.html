Temples in Andhra pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is located in South India and is home to Telugu people. There are many famous temples in Andhra Pradesh, and one of the richest temples in the world, Tirupati Venkateswara Swamy Temple, is also located in this state. Read this article to know more about the famous temples in Andhra Pradesh. This Andhra region was ruled by many dynasties who have built numerous temples that stand still till date. Among these are the temples built during the reign of Sri Krishna Devaraya and Kakatiya dynasty, who took the skill of architecture to pinnacles. These holy abodes are embodiments of Indian heritage and culture. They display exquisitely carved sculptures and structures, which are popular across the world and have attracted a great number of tourists.
